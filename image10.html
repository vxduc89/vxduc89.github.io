VOYAGER MASK SNORKEL FIN SET

DESCRIPTION:
With an array of innovative features packed into each product, this trio of adventure necessities is everything you need for underwater excitement. Voyager fins maintain a closed pocket design for improved foot support. Upward turned edges eliminate discomfort while side foot vents enable drainage and treaded soles increase grip. Also constructed with Hydroflex technology, the LVMSF snorkel's Liquid Silicone tube flexes without kinking for optimized airflow.
The unabridged anterior of the Voyager mask delivers extended peripheral vision for 360 degree, panoramic optics and enhanced underwater surveillance.
