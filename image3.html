CONVOY TRANSITION BACKPACK

DESCRIPTION:
The Convoy Transition Backpack is the Triathlon Bag that we wished for when we were cussing out slow zippers and wrong-sized compartments crammed with gear at transition. Designed to make life easier and more organized for triathletes, the LTRX showcases a 30" x 15" x 15" design and metal frame construction for rugged durability. A front stretch mesh helmet compartment can accommodate all helmet types and super-strong nylon construction is designed to last. Padding throughout the lower back, mid back, shoulders and obliques helps to distribute weight, while padded waist buckles provide accessible storage.
Because races are won by seconds, the Convoy Transition Bag opens like a tool box so you don't have to spend time digging. Three internal mesh dividers separate swim, bike and run gear, and multiple internal pockets allow for custom gear configurations. A removable wetsuit bag keeps your gear dry, while two external water bottle pockets and zippered compartments keep nutrition and hydration at your finger tips.
Dimensions: 30"H x 15"W x 15"D (75 Liters)
